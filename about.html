Our Story

Founded in Kingston, Jamaica, ZenLight Candle Co. was born from a love of peaceful living and mindful design. Our founder believed that a candle is more than just light — it’s an experience that transforms space, mood, and energy.

Mission

To create eco-conscious products that bring serenity and light into every home.

Vision

To become Jamaica’s leading sustainable candle brand recognized for craftsmanship, wellness, and balance.

Slogan

“Light Your Calm.”

Our Values

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and natural ingredients to protect the environment and promote mindfulness.

Craftsmanship

Each candle is hand-poured with precision and love to ensure the highest quality experience.

Wellness

Our scents are designed to calm your mind, ease stress, and transform your home into a sanctuary.

Bring Zen Home
